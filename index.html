About the Founder

The Man Behind the Mission

Rahul Mukherjee

Founder, Wowspire Media

With over a decade in travel, hospitality, and digital marketing, I've had the privilege of working with some of India's biggest names — MakeMyTrip, Yatra, and Goibibo — before building something of my own.

As the founder of RustickKraft, I secured funding from Facebook and built a partnership with Disney, proving that bold ideas backed by strategy can open doors most people think are closed.

I've led OTA operations at the Director level for Hahalolo, an international social travel platform, and hold an MBA from Jain University where my research focused on digital transformation in the travel industry.

But through it all, one thing became clear — most businesses don't need more marketing. They need the RIGHT marketing. That's why I built Wowspire. Every business is unique, and at Wowspire, every brand gets a strategy built just for them.
